    ABOUT

    LISP was created in 1958 by John McCarthy. John McCarthy was a computer scientist at MIT. Lisp was initially developed for AI research as mathematical notation. It is the second oldest high level programming language.

    LISP is used for researching AI, natural lamguage processing, and symbolic computing. It is often used for prototyping, experimentation and rapid development because of its flexibility in handling data structures.

    LISPs usage has declined over time. It still remains an influential language.
